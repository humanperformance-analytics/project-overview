# Non-Medical Use Statement

Human Performance Analytics Project is not a medical device and is not intended to provide medical diagnosis, treatment, prescription, disease detection or clinical decision-making.

The project is designed for non-diagnostic wellness and performance analytics.

## Intended Outputs

Outputs may include:

- longitudinal trends;
- training load indicators;
- sleep trends;
- recovery-related signals;
- data quality indicators;
- contextual performance insights;
- nutrition-context summaries.

## Not Intended For

The project is not intended to:

- diagnose a medical condition;
- treat a medical condition;
- prescribe medication or treatment;
- replace a medical consultation;
- predict injuries with certainty;
- make clinical decisions.

## Interpretation

All insights should be interpreted as contextual, non-diagnostic and dependent on data quality.

When data are incomplete or uncertain, limitations and confidence levels should be displayed.

## Professional Advice

Users with medical concerns, symptoms or persistent pain should seek advice from a qualified professional.
